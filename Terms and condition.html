Terms and Condition

Online Submission Of Form

  1. The detail of degree programs being offered during the Academic Session, 2020-21, and minimum eligibility criteria of each is given separately. However, the University reserves the right to revise any of the eligibility criteria at any stage without assigning any reason.
  2. All the prospective candidates will apply for admission online. After Qualifying Entry Test (for admission to degree programs offered by the Faculty of Agri. Engineering & Technology) and those who want to seek admission in other degree programs will log in to the admission portal by using username and password (already registered by the candidate himself).
  3. Now, print voucher for admission processing fee and submit the fee in the designated Bank (printed on the slip). The admission processing fee will be Rs. 1000/- (to apply for five degree programmes) at the main campus or sub-campuses. To apply for each five additional degree programmes, the candidate will have to pay additional Rs. 1000/-. To apply for Evening program (self-finance), the admission processing fee will be Rs. 500/- in addition to above fee.
  4. Candidate district/Residential category will be based on matric school address mentioned on matric certificate.
  5. Candidate need to submit semester dues before due date otherwise he/she will lose the right of admission

Entry Test

  1. Due to COVID-19 Pandemic and on the direction of the Government of Pakistan to maintain the social distance, UAF has decided to waive off Entry Test for all degree programs except the degrees offered in the Faculty of Agricultural Engineering and Technology
  2. Only one Entry Test will be conducted according to the notified schedule at Main Campus and its three Sub-Campuses for admission to degree courses offered by the Faculty of Engineering & Technology.
  3. The test will be from the Syllabus of Intermediate (Part-I&II) of the test stream (English, Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics) approved by the University. The detail of degree programs being offered during the next Session, 2020-2021, minimum eligibility criteria for each is given separately.
  4. Before opening on-line registration portal, candidate should keep original/copy of Computerized National Identity Card or its number, Secondary School Certificate, Intermediate (Part-I) result card and one colored photograph.
